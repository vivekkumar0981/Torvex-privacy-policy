# Torvex Privacy Policy

**Last updated: February 20, 2026**

This Privacy Policy explains how **Torvex** (“we”, “us”) collects, uses, and shares information when you use the Torvex mobile application (the “App”).

---

## 1. Overview

This Privacy Policy applies to the Torvex app and describes our practices regarding the information we collect, use, and disclose in connection with the App.

---

## 2. Information You Provide

The App stores information you enter, such as:

- Routine names, schedules, reminders, and notes  
- If you sign in, we may also store basic profile details such as your email address and name (if you provide it)  
- Family and sharing-related data you choose to add (e.g. family members, access keys)

---

## 3. Information Collected Automatically

The App and our service providers may collect technical information such as device and app identifiers, IP address, crash logs, and usage events. This helps us keep the App reliable and improve performance.

---

## 4. Ads and Advertising Identifiers

The App does not show ads at this time and does not use advertising identifiers for advertising or attribution.

---

## 5. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Provide core features (such as reminders and sync)  
- Maintain security and troubleshoot issues  
- Personalize your experience  

---

## 6. Sharing

We may share information with:

- **Service providers** that help operate the App (such as Google Firebase for authentication and storage)  
- **Authorities or others** when required by law or to protect users and the App  

---

## 7. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain data as long as needed to provide the App. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us. Some information may be retained where required by law or for legitimate business purposes (for example, security or fraud prevention).

---

## 8. Security

We use reasonable safeguards designed to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage over the internet or on a device is 100% secure.

---

## 9. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age required by local law to consent to data processing. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us so we can request or process deletion as appropriate.

---

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will indicate the “Last updated” date at the top. Your continued use of the App after an update means you accept the updated policy.

---
