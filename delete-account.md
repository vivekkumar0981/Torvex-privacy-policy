# Delete Account (Torvex)

This page explains how to delete your Torvex account and associated data.

## Delete your account in the app (recommended)
1. Open **Torvex**
2. Tap **Profile**
3. Open **Settings**
4. Tap **Account Settings**
5. Under **Danger Zone**, tap **Delete My Account**
6. Confirm both prompts to permanently delete the account

## What gets deleted
When you delete your account, we delete:
- Your **account profile** data (name, email, phone, avatar if stored)
- Your **routines** and schedules
- Your **activity history** / tracking data
- Your **family circle / access relationships** (where applicable)
- Any private app data stored for your account

## What may be retained
We may retain limited information only when required for legal, security, fraud-prevention, or compliance purposes (if applicable).

## Timeframe
- In-app deletion: typically **immediate**
- Some backups/logs (if any) may take up to **30 days** to fully expire

## Need help?
If you cannot access the app and need account deletion support, contact us:
- Email: **torvexapp@gmail.com**
- Subject: **Account Deletion Request**
- Include the email/phone you used to sign in

We may ask for verification to prevent unauthorized deletion.
